How to Buy
If you use a mobile phone to buy:

Open the Duck dApp site in your wallet browser.

Then click the Connect Wallet button. (Duck Dao dApp site)

Confirm that your wallet connects to the site.

If all the steps are done correctly, now your site and wallet are connected and you can make your buy. (Follow the next steps to buy)

Select the amount of your capital to buy the token (BNB)

Click on the Buy token button.

If you want to get only free AirDrop, click on the Claim Airdrop button.

In the window that opens in your wallet, confirm the buy transaction.

If you have BNB balance, the transaction will be successful and a token will be sent to you. (Tokens are sent instantly)
